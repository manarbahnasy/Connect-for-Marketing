Our Story

Founded by Mr. Ashraf Bahnasy, Connect Company for Brokerage & Marketing builds on more than 35 years of experience in the food and feed industries. Between 1989 and 2003, Mr. Bahnasy worked in the food trade across Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, Yemen, and Egypt, gaining deep insights into regional market dynamics. Since 2003, the company has specialized in the feed ingredients sector, forming strong partnerships with leading producers of fishmeal, fish oil, and poultry by-products. Since 2012, Connect has proudly represented two of the world’s top American feed trading companies, along with partners from Bulgaria, Mexico, India, Vietnam, Singapore, and Taiwan. Guided by professionalism, integrity, and long-term cooperation, we are dedicated to building lasting relationships that deliver real value to our global partners.

Our Mission - Our Vision

  • Our Mission

To provide reliable international brokerage services in feed raw materials — connecting producers and buyers through transparent trade, efficient logistics, and sustained partnerships that create mutual value.

  • Our Vision

To become a trusted global trading platform in the animal feed sector, recognized for reliability, integrity, and linking quality producers with world markets.
